Pricing FAQ

Are the prices fixed or can they be negotiated?

+

Our standard solutions (Web-based System & Mobile Application) are fixed at 500,000 TZS each. Custom packages (Basic, Standard, Premier) are also fixed-price based on the package selected. For enterprise projects with specific requirements, we offer custom quotes.

What's included in the support period?

+

Support includes bug fixes, minor updates, technical assistance, and guidance on using your system. Support period varies by package: Standard Solutions (3 months), Basic (2 months), Standard (4 months), and Premier (6 months).

Can I upgrade from Basic to Standard or Premier later?

+

Yes, you can upgrade your package at any time. We'll apply the price difference and extend your support period accordingly. Contact us to discuss upgrade options.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept Mobile Money (M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money), Bank Transfers (TZS), and Cash payments. For international clients, we accept Bank Transfers (SWIFT) and major Credit Cards.

How long does development take?

+

Development timeline depends on the package and project complexity: Standard Solutions (4-6 weeks), Basic Package (6-8 weeks), Standard Package (8-12 weeks), and Premier Package (12-16 weeks). Timeline begins after project kickoff and requirements finalization.
